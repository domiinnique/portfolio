BASIC CONTENT

05.01.01: A story without words

In this assignment in a small group I had to create "a story without words" - a short video story without any plot-driving words in it. We chose to shoot the process of a person decorating the Christmas tree. Our aim was to portray true Christmas spirit and tell a story not only without using words but also without any facial expression since our main "character" is just a pair of feet.

05.02.01: Foodsharing Copenhagen

Final task was to create a campaign site for a client - Foodsharing Copenhagen. In this assignment I implemented everything I learned so far - content creation, web design, user experience, SVG animations. My group focused on a problem of food waste introducing it from the viewer's point of view by showing different examples of food which is about to be thrown out and then showing tips on how to use them. Our theme is "Don't judge a book by its cover". Video shows that produce which doesn't look spotless is still perfectly edible, useful and benefitial to people. In this group project my main role was focused on coding the website.
